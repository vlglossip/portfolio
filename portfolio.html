Resume

This website began as a class project and has since evolved into a comprehensive online portfolio that showcases my professional journey and educational contributions. It highlights my work experience, volunteer service, and educational background, along with direct access to my YouTube channel, my first book A Girl's Blueprint to Building Websites, and my Amazon author page. Serving as both a resource and a resume, the site embodies my commitment to empowering others in tech and education.

Sprout & Grow

'Sprout & Grow' is a website designed for a fictional plant nursery located in North Carolina. It offers visitors access to informational resources, promotional deals, a catalog of available plants, and details about community and educational events promoting organic gardening. Visitors interested in these events can sign up online through the site.

Meal Planner

'Meal Planner' is a website aimed at helping you plan your meals for the week. It has room for you to plan breakfast, lunch, dinner, and two snacks. If you'd like to lose weight, but don't have any healthy food ideas, the pre-written examples are a perfect starter! If you're an experienced healthy eater, you can use the 'clear all' button and write in your own meals for the week. Once you're happy with your meal plan, you're promped to put in some personal information that's on the printable output you recieve when you submit.
