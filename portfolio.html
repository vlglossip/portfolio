Sprout & Grow

Sprout & Grow is a dynamic, user-centered website I designed for a fictional plant nursery based in North Carolina. The site serves as an interactive platform that blends commerce with education, offering a detailed plant catalog, promotional deals, and valuable resources for organic gardening enthusiasts. In addition to showcasing plant varieties, the website promotes community and educational events, enabling visitors to sign up for workshops and classes focused on sustainable gardening practices. Through this project, I highlighted my skills in web design, user experience, and content management while fostering a deeper connection between the nursery and its local community.

Meal Planner

Meal Planner is a user-friendly website designed to help individuals plan their meals for the week, promoting healthy eating habits and lifestyle. The site provides sections for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and two snacks, offering pre-written meal suggestions for those looking to lose weight or maintain a healthy diet. For more experienced users, there’s an option to clear all the pre-written content and input personalized meals. Once the user finalizes their meal plan, the site prompts them to input personal information, which is included in the printable output generated upon submission. This project showcases my ability to design practical, interactive websites that cater to specific user needs, ensuring both functionality and a seamless user experience.
