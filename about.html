Victoria is certified in both computer programming and web development. Driven by a lifelong passion for coding and a commitment to inspiring others, she founded the Bit by Bit Coding Club at her former middle school and launched the HHS WiSTEM Chapter at her current high school. Victoria's local successes propelled her to expand her initiative globally where she leads outreach efforts at CODEify, helping boost student enrollment by over 203.85% across 15 countries. As the youngest Junior Developer Intern at Farragut Systems, she has worked on groundbreaking Python-based applications and contributed to a product that will revolutionize real estate valuation. In addition, she has authored A Girl’s Blueprint to Building Websites and launched a YouTube channel to extend free programming lessons to global audiences. Her goal is to continue expanding opportunities in tech education and shape the future of technology. Through her work with CODEify, Bit by Bit, and WiSTEM, she's reached over 16,500 individuals.
